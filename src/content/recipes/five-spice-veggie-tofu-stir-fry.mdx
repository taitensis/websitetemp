ingredient_groups:
  - ingredients:
      - 1 medium zucchini, sliced
      - 1 capsicum (bell pepper), sliced
      - 1 carrot, coarsely grated
      - 200 g firm tofu, pressed & cubed
      - 1 tbsp cornstarch (for tofu)
      - 2 tbsp neutral oil (e.g. canola or peanut)
      - 2 cloves garlic, minced
      - 1 tbsp grated ginger (optional)
      - 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
      - 2 tbsp soy sauce
      - 1 tbsp lime juice
      - 1 tsp sugar
      - 1 tsp sesame oil (for finishing)
steps:
  - Pat tofu dry, cube it, toss in cornstarch. Pan-fry in oil until golden; set aside.
  - In the same wok, sauté garlic and ginger for 30 sec to 1 min.
  - Add five-spice, stir briefly. Add zucchini and capsicum; stir-fry 2–3 min.
  - Add grated carrot and cook another 2 min, until just tender.
  - Return tofu; add soy sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Toss to coat.
  - Drizzle with sesame oil, toss, and serve hot.
notes:
  - Serve over rice or noodles.
  - Add chili flakes or chili oil if you like heat.
  - Use mushrooms or snap peas for variety.
